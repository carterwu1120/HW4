This data involves students using Dynamic Composite Physicalizations to enhance learning performance.

Dynamic composite physicalizations refer to a category of physical visualizations that use self-propelled objects in collections to represent data. These visualizations can embody well-known interactive visualization techniques and explore novel visualizations and interaction paradigms.
